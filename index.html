Algorithm Transparency

Default fair mode: every eligible person has exactly one ticket. Winners are chosen with unbiased rejection sampling using crypto.getRandomValues(). This avoids the bias that can happen with Math.random() or modulo-based random picks.

Weighted mode: optional and disclosed. It calculates an average engagement score from numeric 1–5 survey answers and customization interest, then gives proportionally more ticket weight. Do not use this mode unless your giveaway rules explicitly allow weighted entries.
